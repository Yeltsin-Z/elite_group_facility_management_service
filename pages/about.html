Our Story

Elite Group Facility Management Service was incorporated in 2009 and started operation in a modest way. Now we have also undertaken to provide A to Z Manpower (Technical & Non-Technical including PSO & Gunman etc) at Commercial sites (Construction / Manufacturing Units) & Domestic Units as per requirement basis.

We spread all over the Chennai region and deputed our employees near about 500+ at commercial sites & domestic units.

With our dedicated team of professionals, we've grown to become one of the leading facility management service providers in the region, known for reliability, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Expert Training Center

We invest in the development of our staff to ensure the highest service quality

All Guards deployed at the site(s) have undergone our detailed Basic Training Module conducted at our Training School. Apart from the Basic Training Module, every Guard also undergoes on site training.

Our training programs cover all aspects of security and facility management, including:

  • Security protocols and emergency response
  • Customer service and professional conduct
  • Equipment operation and maintenance
  • Health and safety procedures
  • Specialized training for different facility types
